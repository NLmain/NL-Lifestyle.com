Oats contain a number of compounds, including polyphenols called avenanthramides, which serve as antioxidants. Avenanthramides may aid in regulating blood pressure by increasing the production of nitric oxide (lower blood pressure and have additional advantages.)

DIABETES

After having a meal, beta-glucan fiber may lessen the likelihood of sudden spikes in blood sugar and insulin levels. Oat may improve gut health because intestinal bacteria break down and ferment the fiber. Oats that have been processed might be included in a diabetic's diet.
